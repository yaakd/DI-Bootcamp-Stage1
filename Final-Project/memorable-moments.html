Memorable Moments

Arnold's iconic line "I'll be back" from the 1984 film "The Terminator" has become one of the most famous movie quotes of all time.

Mr. Freeze - Batman & Robin (1997)

Arnold played the supervillain Mr. Freeze in "Batman & Robin" (1997), delivering memorable one-liners like "Ice to meet you!" and "Everybody, chill!"

Conan the Librarian - "Weird Al" Yankovic's "UHF" (1989)

Arnold was parodied as "Conan the Librarian" in "Weird Al" Yankovic's 1989 comedy film "UHF."

Arnold and the Mars Atmosphere - Total Recall (1990)

In the 1990 sci-fi film "Total Recall," Arnold's character, Douglas Quaid, experiences a dramatic decompression scene on the surface of Mars.

Arnold as a Pregnant Man - Junior (1994)

In the 1994 comedy film "Junior," Arnold played a scientist who becomes pregnant as part of a groundbreaking medical experiment, resulting in some hilarious moments.

Arnold Versus the Predator - Predator (1987)

In the 1987 action film "Predator," Arnold's character, Dutch, engages in a thrilling battle against a deadly alien hunter in the jungle.

Arnold's Austrian Army Tank - The Last Stand (2013)

Arnold drives an Austrian Army tank in the 2013 film "The Last Stand," giving a nod to his time serving in the Austrian Army before his bodybuilding career took off.
